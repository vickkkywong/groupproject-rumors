Details of Rumors

The cause of Rumors

    1.Incomplete or Wrong Information:
    may hear only part of an event or hear someone else's interpretation and then spread it without fact-checking. This inaccuracy in messaging can lead to the creation and spread of rumors.
    2.Social media and the Internet:
    People can easily share information with others through social media and messaging apps, which allows rumors to spread quickly in a short period of time.
    3.Lack of credible sources:
    In some situations, people may lack credible sources, especially in emergencies or uncertain situations.
    4.Personal biases and preferences:
    If a rumor fits someone's existing beliefs or biases, they are more likely to accept and spread it without thinking deeply about it or fact-checking it.
